About Us

Come find us at 931C Mount Eden Road!

Welcome to Sunrise Bakery, a cherished family-owned establishment nestled next to Cheese on Toast, you can find a classic New Zealand bakery. We take pride in our pastries and sandwiches! We also offer a variety of sweets like biscuits and banana bread slices.
